Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I get my restaurant website online?

Most restaurants can have their website live within 24 hours using our templates and guided setup process.

Do you provide training for my staff?

Yes! We offer comprehensive training sessions for your team, including video tutorials and live training calls for Pro and Enterprise customers.

Can you help migrate from my current system?

Absolutely. Our migration specialists will help transfer your existing data, including menus, customer information, and order history.

What if I need custom features?

Our Enterprise plan includes custom development options. We can build specific features tailored to your restaurant's unique needs.

Is my data secure with RestaurantTech?

Yes, we use bank-level encryption and are PCI DSS compliant. Your data and your customers' information are completely secure.
